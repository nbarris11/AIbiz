A dedicated AI strategist embedded in your business — without the cost of a full-time hire. Strategy, oversight, and hands-on support, month after month.

Book a Free Discovery Call

AI doesn't stop after the first project

Most AI wins don't come from a single big implementation. They come from a dozen small, compounding improvements made over time — optimized prompts, new tool integrations, team adoption, and constant iteration as the technology itself evolves.

The Fractional AI Advisor program gives you an expert in your corner, every month, to make sure that keeps happening.

What a fractional AI advisor does for you

Think of it as having a senior AI strategist on your team part-time. Every month, we:

  • Review what's working and what's breaking in your current AI stack
  • Identify new opportunities as your business changes and AI tools evolve
  • Oversee implementation of new automations and integrations
  • Coach your team on using AI tools effectively
  • Stay ahead of relevant AI developments so you don't have to
  • Serve as a sounding board for any AI-related decisions

What's included each month

  • Monthly strategy call — 60-minute deep-dive to review progress, set priorities, and plan the next 30 days
  • Async Slack or email support — quick questions answered within one business day
  • Monthly written brief — summary of what was done, what's next, and any AI developments relevant to your business
  • Implementation hours — hands-on build time for workflows, prompts, and integrations (hours vary by tier)
  • Quarterly business review — in-depth assessment of ROI, strategic direction, and next quarter's roadmap

Who this is for

The Fractional AI Advisor program is the right fit if:

  • You've completed an AI Clarity Session and want help executing the plan
  • You're already using AI tools but know you could be doing more with them
  • You're growing and need a strategic partner who understands AI deeply
  • You can't justify a full-time AI hire but want consistent, expert-level support
  • You want someone accountable for AI outcomes in your business, not just a consultant who disappears

How it's different from a one-time project

One-time implementations solve today's problems. The fractional model solves them continually. AI tools evolve monthly. Your business evolves too. A fractional advisor keeps you from falling behind — and builds on each win to compound results over time.

Most clients see meaningful ROI within the first 60–90 days. After six months, the time savings, reduced errors, and improved output quality typically far exceed the retainer cost.

Frequently asked questions

What's the minimum commitment?

We ask for a 3-month minimum to allow time for real results to develop. After that, the engagement is month-to-month.

How many hours per month does this include?

It depends on the tier. The entry tier ($1,500/mo) includes approximately 4 hours of hands-on work per month plus unlimited async support. The growth tier ($3,000/mo) includes approximately 10 hours plus priority response times. We scope the right tier during the discovery call.

Do I need to have done a Clarity Session first?

Not required, but recommended. The Clarity Session gives us the foundation — the audit, the plan, and the priorities — so we're not starting from scratch. If you haven't done one, we'll build that assessment into the first month of the retainer.

What if I need more work in a given month?

Additional hours are available at a discounted project rate for retainer clients. We'll always flag it and get your approval before going over.

Can I pause or cancel?

After the initial 3-month period, you can pause or cancel with 30 days' notice. No long-term lock-ins.
