AI Implementation

We don't just tell you what to build — we build it. Done-for-you AI workflows, automations, and integrations, scoped and delivered end to end.

Book a Free Discovery Call

Knowing what to build is step one. Building it is step two.

Most small business owners don't have time to implement AI themselves — even when they know exactly what they need. The AI Implementation service handles everything: design, configuration, testing, and handoff. You stay focused on running your business.

What we build

Every project is scoped to your specific situation, but common implementations include:

  • AI-assisted customer communication — email drafting, inquiry response, follow-up sequences
  • Document and proposal automation — quotes, contracts, intake forms, SOPs
  • Internal knowledge bases — AI-searchable repositories of your processes, products, and FAQs
  • Scheduling and admin workflows — automated booking, reminders, status updates
  • Content and marketing automation — AI-assisted blog posts, social captions, email newsletters
  • Custom GPTs or AI assistants — trained on your business data, ready for your team
  • Tool integrations — connecting your CRM, calendar, email, and project management tools via AI-powered workflows

How the process works

AI Implementation projects follow a clear four-phase process:

  • Scoping (Week 1) — We review your AI Clarity Session plan (or conduct a fast-track audit) and define the exact scope, deliverables, and timeline. You receive a fixed quote before any work begins.
  • Build (Weeks 2–4) — We design and build the workflows, integrations, or tools. You receive weekly updates and can give feedback throughout.
  • Testing & refinement (Week 4–5) — We test everything in your real environment, fix any issues, and tune for accuracy and reliability.
  • Handoff & training (Week 5–6) — We hand over documentation, train your team, and stay available for two weeks of post-launch support.

What it costs

Projects are fixed-scope with fixed pricing — no hourly billing surprises. Most projects fall in the $2,500–$8,000 range depending on complexity. A simple single-workflow build is at the low end. Multi-tool integrations or custom AI assistants are at the high end. You receive a written quote after scoping and approve it before we start.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need technical skills to work with you?

None. We handle all the technical work. You need to be available for calls, feedback, and the training session — that's it.

What tools do you work with?

We work with the major AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini), automation tools (Zapier, Make, n8n), and common small business software (HubSpot, Notion, Google Workspace, QuickBooks, and more). If you use a tool we haven't worked with, we'll tell you upfront.

What if I need changes after the project ends?

Minor adjustments within the two-week post-launch window are included. Larger changes or new features can be scoped as a follow-on project, or handled through our Fractional AI Advisor retainer.

How is pricing determined?

We scope each project individually based on the number of workflows, the tools involved, the complexity of integrations, and the amount of training required. We provide a written fixed quote before any work begins — no surprises.
