AI Clarity Session

A 90-minute deep-dive into your business. You leave with a prioritized written action plan — and clarity on exactly where AI can help.

The problem most small businesses face

You've heard AI can save you time. You've seen the headlines. Maybe you've even tried ChatGPT a few times. But you have no idea where to actually start — and you don't have hours to figure it out.

The AI Clarity Session solves exactly that. In 90 minutes, we do the thinking for you.

What happens in an AI Clarity Session

Before the session, we send a short pre-work form asking about your current tools, your biggest time drains, and your goals. This lets us make the 90 minutes as useful as possible.

During the session (video call), we:

  • Walk through your core workflows — how you get work, do work, and follow up
  • Identify where you're losing the most time and money to manual tasks
  • Map your top 3–5 AI opportunities ranked by impact and ease of implementation
  • Answer every question you have about AI, tools, and whether it makes sense for your business

Within 48 hours of the session, you receive a written action plan outlining exactly what to do, in what order, and roughly what it will cost.

What you walk away with

  • A clear map of your highest-value AI opportunities
  • A prioritized implementation roadmap (what to do first, second, third)
  • Specific tool recommendations with rationale
  • Realistic time and cost estimates for each opportunity
  • A written plan you can act on — with us or on your own

Who this is for

The AI Clarity Session is ideal if:

  • You're curious about AI but don't know where to start
  • You've tried a few tools but nothing has stuck
  • You want a second opinion on whether AI is worth it for your business
  • You're about to hire or expand and want to see if AI can help you scale smarter
  • You're simply losing too many hours to tasks that feel repetitive

What it is not

The Clarity Session is not a sales call for our other services. You will not be pressured to buy anything else. The written plan is yours regardless of what you decide to do next. Many clients complete a Clarity Session and implement the plan on their own — and we think that's great.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need technical skills?

None. The session is a conversation about your business, not a technical workshop. We translate everything into plain language.

What if I'm already using some AI tools?

Even better. We'll evaluate what you have, tell you what's working and what isn't, and identify the gaps worth filling.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes. If you don't feel the written action plan was worth your investment, we'll refund you in full.

What happens after the session?

You receive your written plan within 48 hours. From there, you choose: implement it yourself, hire someone else to do it, or work with Sidecar on our AI Implementation service. No pressure either way.
