What Is an AI Clarity Session?

Most small businesses don't know where to start with AI — and that uncertainty costs time and money. An AI Clarity Session gives you a concrete answer in 90 minutes, backed by a written plan.

April 2026 · 6 min read · By Sidecar Advisory

The hardest part of adopting AI as a small business isn't the technology — it's the paralysis. There are hundreds of tools available, everyone has an opinion, and the stakes feel high enough that most owners end up doing nothing while waiting for the "right" moment to start. That moment rarely comes on its own.

An AI Clarity Session is designed to break that paralysis. In a single focused engagement, you go from "I know I should be using AI but I don't know where to start" to a specific, prioritized, written plan for your exact business. Here's what it involves and what you can expect.

The Core Idea

An AI Clarity Session is a paid, 90-minute deep-dive into your business operations. The goal is simple: identify where AI can have the biggest measurable impact on your business right now, and hand you a plan to get there.

It's not a sales pitch. It's not a generic overview of AI tools. It's a structured audit of your specific workflows, bottlenecks, and goals — followed by a written action plan delivered within 48 hours that you can act on whether you continue working with us or not.

What Happens Before the Session

A Clarity Session starts before you even get on a call. Once you book, you'll receive a short pre-session questionnaire that asks you to describe your main workflows, where you spend the most time, what tools you currently use, and what outcomes matter most to you.

This prep work isn't busywork. It lets us come into the session already familiar with your business, which means the 90 minutes is spent on analysis and discussion — not introductions. We review your responses in advance and identify the areas most worth exploring together.

What Happens During the Session

The session itself is a structured conversation, not a lecture. We walk through your operations area by area — customer communication, scheduling, marketing, admin, reporting — and probe for the workflows that are taking the most time or generating the most friction.

We ask questions that most business owners haven't been asked before. Not "what tools do you use?" but "what do you do the same way every single week?" Not "are you interested in AI?" but "if you could eliminate one task from your week, what would it be?" These questions surface the opportunities that aren't obvious from the outside.

By the end of the session, you'll have talked through every major area of your business and have a clear sense of where the highest-value AI opportunities are. We also do a quick sanity check on feasibility — not every good idea is worth pursuing first, and part of our job is helping you sequence things in a way that makes sense.

What You Get Afterward

Within 48 hours of the session, you receive a written action plan. This document isn't a generic AI overview — it's specific to your business. It includes:

  • Your top 3–5 AI opportunities, ranked by estimated impact and ease of implementation
  • Specific tool recommendations for each opportunity, with notes on why they fit your situation
  • Time and cost estimates so you know what each opportunity requires before you commit
  • A suggested sequencing — what to do first, second, and later
  • Implementation notes for the first opportunity, so you have a concrete starting point

This document is yours. You can take it to another provider, implement it yourself, or use it as the foundation for working with us further. There's no lock-in, and the plan is designed to be actionable regardless of what you decide next.

What It Costs — and the Guarantee

An AI Clarity Session costs $300–$500 depending on the complexity of your business and how much pre-session preparation is involved. We offer a straightforward money-back guarantee: if you don't feel the session delivered clear, actionable value for your business, you don't pay.

That guarantee matters because it reflects how we think about the engagement. The session only works if we actually understand your business and give you recommendations you can use. If we miss the mark, we haven't earned the fee.

How It Fits Into the Bigger Picture

For most small businesses, an AI Clarity Session is the right starting point — before any tool purchase, before any implementation, before any ongoing advisory engagement. It gives you the map before you start the journey.

Many clients use the written action plan to implement their first AI tool on their own and see results within 30 days. Others move directly into our AI Implementation service, where we handle the setup, configuration, and testing ourselves. And for businesses that want ongoing support as the AI landscape evolves, the Clarity Session naturally leads into our Fractional AI Advisor program.

But none of that is required. The session stands on its own. The goal is to give you clarity — hence the name.

The most expensive AI decision a small business can make is spending months on the wrong tool. A Clarity Session costs a fraction of that and gets you pointed in the right direction from the start.

How to Book One

Every AI Clarity Session starts with a free 30-minute discovery call. This call lets us make sure we're the right fit for your business and gives you a chance to ask questions before committing. There's no pitch — just a conversation about your business and what you're hoping AI can do for it.

If it makes sense to move forward, we'll schedule the Clarity Session and send you the pre-session questionnaire. From there, the whole process — discovery call, Clarity Session, written plan — takes less than two weeks from start to finish.
