Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about AI for your small business.

Honest answers — no jargon, no hype. If your question isn't here, just ask us.

What is an AI Clarity Session?

An AI Clarity Session is a 90-minute paid deep-dive into your business. We map your current workflows, identify where you're losing the most time and money, pinpoint your top 3–5 AI opportunities, and deliver a prioritized written action plan within 48 hours.

It costs $300–$500 and is the fastest way to know exactly where AI can help your business — no fluff, no upsell pressure. Whether or not you continue working with us, you leave with a clear, actionable plan.

How much does AI consulting cost for a small business?

Sidecar Advisory is priced for small business budgets, not enterprise ones:

  • AI Clarity Session: $300–$500 (one-time, 90 min + written plan)
  • AI Implementation: $1,500–$5,000 per project (scope-dependent)
  • Fractional AI Advisor: $500–$1,500/month (ongoing support)
  • Workshops & Training: $75–$150 per person

Most clients start with the Clarity Session before committing to anything larger. It's the lowest-risk way to see how we work and whether AI is a good fit for your business right now.

What is a Fractional AI Advisor?

A Fractional AI Advisor is an on-call AI expert who works with your business part-time on an ongoing monthly basis — like having a Chief AI Officer without the full-time salary or commitment.

At Sidecar Advisory, this means: monthly strategy check-ins, proactive recommendations as new AI tools emerge, staff training as needed, and priority support when something breaks or a new opportunity appears. Most clients on this plan spend 2–4 hours per month working with us.

Do I need technical skills to work with Sidecar Advisory?

None at all. We work exclusively with small business owners who have zero technical background — that's the whole point of Sidecar.

We handle every technical aspect: tool selection, account setup, configuration, integration, testing, and training. You tell us what you want to achieve; we figure out how to make it happen. If you can use email and a smartphone, you can work with us.

How long does AI implementation take?

It depends on what you're implementing:

  • Simple automations (auto-replies, appointment reminders, lead follow-ups): 3–7 days
  • Full implementation projects: 2–6 weeks from kickoff to handoff
  • AI Clarity Session action plan: delivered within 48 hours of your session

We don't drag projects out. Most clients see their first measurable time savings within 30 days of starting implementation.

What kinds of small businesses do you work with?

We work with service-based small businesses across most industries: contractors, restaurants, salons and spas, consultants, retail shops, real estate professionals, healthcare practices, law firms, and more.

The common thread is this: if you spend significant time on repetitive tasks — scheduling, follow-ups, invoicing, reporting, customer communications — there is almost certainly an AI opportunity worth exploring.

What happens on the free discovery call?

The free 30-minute discovery call is a conversation — not a sales pitch. We ask about your business, your biggest time drains, your current tools, and your goals. We share honest thoughts on whether AI makes sense for you right now.

There is no obligation to book anything after. Many people leave with 2–3 ideas they can start on immediately, whether they work with us or not. If it's not a good fit, we'll tell you.

What AI tools do you use?

We are tool-agnostic — we recommend what fits your business, not what we're paid to sell. Common tools we work with include ChatGPT, Claude, Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat), GoHighLevel, Notion AI, and various industry-specific platforms.

We always start with what you already use and add only what is genuinely useful. We don't create dependency on exotic tools you can't manage yourself.

Will AI replace my employees?

In our experience, AI does not replace people in small businesses — it makes them faster and less burned out. We focus on automating the tasks nobody enjoys: data entry, report generation, routine email responses, appointment reminders, invoice follow-ups.

Your team gets to focus on work that actually requires a human — relationships, judgment calls, creative work. Most of our clients tell us morale improves after implementation, not the opposite.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

If you complete an AI Clarity Session and don't feel the action plan was worth your investment, we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.

For implementation projects, we do not consider a project complete until you have verified that the tools are working as agreed. We stay in it until it's right. We don't invoice for work that didn't deliver.
